Super Meat Lasagna Recipe

To make a delicious meat lasagna, start by browning the sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef in a large pan over medium heat. Add the minced onion and crushed garlic, cooking until the onion is soft. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, canned tomato sauce, and water. Season with white sugar, dried basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, salt, black pepper, and fresh parsley. Simmer the sauce for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Meanwhile, cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions and set aside. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and a little salt. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, then a layer of the ricotta mixture. Sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a generous topping of cheese. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes, then remove the foil and bake for another 25 minutes until the top is golden and bubbly. Let it rest for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
